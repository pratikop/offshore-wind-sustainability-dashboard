# Methodology

## Project Purpose

This project analyses publicly available RWE sustainability data from 2025 and converts it into a simple dashboard-ready KPI dataset.

The aim is to demonstrate sustainability data analysis, ESG-style KPI tracking, Excel/Power BI dashboarding, and structured project documentation.

## Data Source

The dataset is based on publicly available RWE Group sustainability reporting for 2025.

Main source documents:

- RWE Annual Report 2025
- RWE Sustainability Key Figures Report 2025
- RWE Group Sustainability Reporting – Data 2025

## Data Scope

The dataset uses Group-level RWE sustainability indicators. It does not represent confidential internal data or project-level offshore wind farm data.

The dataset focuses on four main sustainability areas:

1. Carbon emissions
2. Waste
3. Materials and circularity
4. Biodiversity targets

## KPI Categories

### Carbon

Carbon KPIs include Scope 1, Scope 2, Scope 3, and total greenhouse gas emissions.

These indicators help show the emissions profile of RWE Group and support basic carbon reporting analysis.

### Waste

Waste KPIs include total waste generated, hazardous waste, non-recycled waste, and waste directed to disposal.

These indicators support analysis of waste management and circular economy performance.

### Materials and Circularity

Material KPIs include total product material weight, steel, concrete, polymers, glass, secondary materials used, and recycled-content indicators.

These indicators help assess resource use and circularity.

### Biodiversity

Biodiversity entries are target-based indicators because biodiversity impact is reported mainly as strategic goals rather than simple annual numeric KPIs.

## Dashboard Plan

The Power BI dashboard will include:

- Carbon emissions overview
- Waste breakdown
- Materials and circularity overview
- KPI cards for key metrics
- Category-based filters
- Source and data-scope notes

## Limitations

- The dataset is based on public reporting only.
- The data is Group-level, not offshore-wind-project-level.
- Some sustainability areas are represented through targets rather than annual performance values.
- The project is for educational and portfolio purposes.
